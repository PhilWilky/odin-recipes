Chinese Chicken Curry

Description

Authentic English takeaway Chinese Chicken Curry fakeaway with Egg fried Rice

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast, small chunks
  • Brown onion
  • couple of peppers
  • Water
  • Chicken curry power
  • Rice
  • Soy Sauce both dark and light
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Chop breast into small chunks if not already
  2. Prep veg and cut into small bits
  3. Gently flash fry and chickena and onions add peppers just before taking off of pan
  4. Add to larget pan, add curry power and water down as needed
  5. Cook rice
  6. Add egg tht has been mixed
  7. Add salt, pepper and MSG if you using it
  8. serve both together
